Ingredients
	1. 100 grams of Cocoa powder
	2. 200 ml of full cream milk
	3. 30 grams of butter
	4. 50 grams of icing sugar

Directions:
	1. Put the butter in a sauce pan and gently heat it until it melts
	2. Pour the milk into the pan and keep stirring until it boils
	3. Add the icing sugar as you gently stir the boiling milk
	4. Slowly add the cocoa powder and keep stirring.
	5. Stirr the mixture until you get a fine heavy brown paste with no cocoa powder lumps
	6. Turn off the heat.
	7. You can consume it hot or chilled.
